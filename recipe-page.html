Jake the Dog's Perfect Sandwich

This isn't just food, it's a spiritual experience!

Packed with everything but the kitchen sink (and maybe that too), it's the only thing that can satisfy a stretchy dog's bottomless hunger.

Ingredients

  • A whole loaf of crusty bread (unsliced)
  • Lobster soul (for flavor!)
  • A whole wheel of cheese
  • 3 slices of last night's leftover pizza (with anchovies)
  • One hard-boiled dragon egg (chicken egg works too but that's boring)
  • A sprinkle of magic (or just paprika. Salty tears work too)

Instructions

  1. Use your stretchy powers to unhinge your jaw - if you're a human, just open really wide.
  2. Layer everything onto the bread in any order, went to Sandwich Ville and the mayor said sandwiches don't care about order.
  3. Smush it all together until ingredients start leaking out the sides. Juicy looking, yummy.
  4. Inhale the entire thing in one bite.
  5. Immediately start thinking about the next sandwich :D
